The Main Idea

Dungeons & Dragons is a roleplaying tabletop game where you have the dungeon master (DM) and the players. The DM creates and narrates a story for the players to play through.

The players make characters that have classes and races, while the DM plays as the non-player characters (NPCs).

Character Options

Players can play with very different things.

Races include:

  1. Elves
  2. Humans
  3. Dwarves
  4. Halflings

Classes include:

  • Fighters
  • Wizards
  • Monks
  • Sorcerers

Ability Scores

Ability scores are measurments of characters physical and mental abilities.
The ability scores are strength, constitution, dexterity, intelligence, wisdom, and charisma.

Strength:

Strength measures you brute force and raw power; it's used for athletics checks and melee attacks.

Dexterity:

Dexterity measures you agility and is used for ranged attacks and acrobatics, slight of hand, and stealth checks.

Constitution:

Constitution measures you hardiness and is used to calculate your health points (HP).
